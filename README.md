# Tirth Sutra

## Vision

To become the world's most trusted and vibrant digital sanctuary for the Hindu spiritual community — a place where devotees, mandirs, saints, and pilgrims connect, grow, and celebrate their faith together, no matter where they are in the world.

## Mission

To empower the Hindu spiritual community by providing a dedicated platform that:

- Bridges devotees with mandirs, sacred pilgrimages, and revered saints through meaningful digital experiences.
- Fosters genuine devotional connections through community-driven content, real-time conversations, and shared spiritual journeys.
- Preserves and promotes the richness of Hindu culture, traditions, and religious teachings for generations to come.
- Makes spirituality accessible to every devotee — whether at a local mandir or anywhere across the globe.

## Tech Stack

### Frontend

- Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
- Service Worker for browser push notifications (PWA support)
- WebRTC for peer-to-peer voice and video calling
- Socket.io client for real-time communication

### Backend

- **Runtime:** Node.js
- **Framework:** Express.js
- **Database:** MongoDB with Mongoose ODM
- **Real-Time:** Socket.io
- **Authentication:** JWT (JSON Web Tokens), Google OAuth 2.0
- **Email:** Nodemailer (SMTP-based email verification)
- **Media Storage:** Cloudinary
- **Push Notifications:** Web Push (VAPID)
